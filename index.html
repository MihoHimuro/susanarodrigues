Susana Rodrigues

Designer and Illustrator



Multidisciplinary designer and illustrator that blends emotion, aesthetics, and usability across digital experiences.

Over the years, I’ve built a multidisciplinary career that blends children’s illustration, graphic design, 2D animation, product customization, UX/UI, and more.

I’ve created artwork for books, games, short films, and worked on personalized products for various occasions.

Motherhood gave me a new creative lens and the time to develop personal projects, such as the narrative game Amor Estiloso, where I took on the roles of art director, writer, and project manager.

I produced relaxing animated videos for YouTube, illustrated the book PeterLuka, and brought the short film A menina do coração verde to life — fulfilling several creative dreams. I’ve collaborated with brands like Magic Mint, Luís Paulo Representações, Helppier and volunteered actively with the animal protection association VivAnimal, where I combined design with social impact.

I’m currently available for new creative challenges in a remote setting. I’m eager to continue crafting visual experiences that move, inspire, and connect.
